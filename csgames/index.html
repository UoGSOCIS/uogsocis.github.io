CS Games 2015

CS Games is an annual programming competition. It is held this year at Sherbrooke University in Quebec. It is an exciting opportunity for undergraduate students to demonstrate their programming skills in representation of Guelph!

The event is open to all University of Guelph undergraduate students in Computer Science & Software Engineering. In the past, registration & hotel cost has been covered by the SOCS department, transportation is the only cost involved.

Registration

If you are interested in registering for the team, send an email to vp@socis.ca with the following info:

  • Subject line: “Name, School Year, CS Games applicant”
  • A blurb about why you want to go to CS Games/how you feel you will contribute to the team
  • Any programming languages you know, and how well you know them (please be honest)

Deadline for people interested is January 1, 2015. Team will be announced around the start of the winter semester. Regular updates can be found on our Facebook group.
