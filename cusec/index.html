CUSEC

CUSEC is an annual conference that takes place in Montreal in January. Over 400 students interested in Software Engineering and Computer Science attend each year. CUSEC aims to expose students to new technologies, to excite them about software development, and to provide opportunities for connecting with peers and potential employers.
