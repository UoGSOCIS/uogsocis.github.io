GCC Tech Talks are events designed to showcase what your fellow students in SoCS are doing and maybe to even share some knowledge. We've got three awesome talks lined up:

If you want to learn how to be one of the awesome people in movies who furiously press buttons on a keyboard while the FBI stands behind them and they find the bad guys location on a map then this is the talk for you!
